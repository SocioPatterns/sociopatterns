<h1>Event description</h1>

[Under construction]

A pilot experiment in <a href="http://www.ospedalebambinogesu.it/">a major Italian pediatric hospital</a>, in a collaboration with the hospital and the <a href="http://www.iss.it/">ISS</a>. The experiment  involves patients, doctors, healthcare workers and visitors, simultaneously collecting contact information and clinical information. We aim at measuring epidemiologically relevant quantities in a real-world healthcare setting.
