The data set contains observational contact data collected for 86 individuals living in a village in rural Malawi. These data were analyzed and published in the paper <a href="https://epjdatascience.springeropen.com/articles/10.1140/epjds/s13688-021-00302-w" target="_blank">Using wearable proximity sensors to characterize social contact patterns in a village of rural Malawi"</a>.
