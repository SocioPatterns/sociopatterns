Ciro Cattuto is presenting a review of our results in the workshop on "Temporal and Dynamic Networks: From Data to Models" taking place on June 3-4, 2013 in Copenhagen, Denmark as a satellite of <a href="https://www.netscisociety.net/events/netsci">NetSci2013</a>. Moreover, Laetitia Gauvin presented new results on "The activity-community structure of time-varying networks: A non-negative tensor factorization approach" in the workshop on "Dynamic Information and Communication Networks 2013".
