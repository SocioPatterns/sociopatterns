Recent results from the SocioPatterns collaboration will be presented at the 3rd <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/events/conferences/all/international-conference-on-infectious-disease-dynamics">Epidemics conference</a> in Boston, USA (Nov. 29th - Dec. 2nd, 2011).

Ciro Cattuto will give a talk titled <em>"Close encounters in a pediatric ward: Measuring face-to-face proximity and mixing patterns with wearable sensors"</em>, and two posters will as well be presented.
