Wouter Van den Broeck, a member of the SocioPatterns project and one of the initiators of the <a href="/deployments/practice-mapping/">Practice Mapping</a> project, will participate in the <a href="https://www.pixelache.ac/events/mapping-in-progress-workshop">Mapping in Progress</a> workshop at the <a href="https://www.pixelache.ac/festivals/festival-2011">Pixelache Festival of electronic art and subcultures</a> in Helsinki as well as give a talk during the <a href="https://www.pixelache.ac/events/map-me-if-you-will-seminar">Map me if you will</a> seminar. We're looking forward to both opportunities for auspicious interaction with the community and hope you will be there as well!
