As each year, SocioPatterns members have presented new results at the <a href="https://www.netscisociety.net/events/netsci">NetSci2014 conference</a> in Berkeley, CA.
Ciro Cattuto was invited speaker at the workshop on "Temporal Networks, Human Dynamics and Social Physics", with a presentation titled "Learning structures from high-resolution social network data". 
Laetitia Gauvin presented results on "Selective Latent Factor Analysis for Epidemic Spreading in Time-Varying Networks" in the main conference.
