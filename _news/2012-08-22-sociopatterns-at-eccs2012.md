New results from the SocioPatterns collaboration will be presented at the <a href="https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-319-00395-5">European Complex Systems Conference 2012</a> in Brussels, Belgium.

Laetitia Gauvin will speak in the main conference track on September 7th about <em>Patterns of spreading dynamics over networks of human proximity</em>.

Anna Machens will speak at the satellite workshop on <a href="http://epifor.isi.it/bb/post/74">Data-Driven Modeling of Contagion Processes</a> about <em>Bridging the gap between dynamic network data and contact matrices</em>.
